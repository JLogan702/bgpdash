Executive Summary

This dashboard reflects team-level metrics around Sprint Readiness and Backlog Health across all 5 squads contributing to the Brand Growth Platform initiative. Metrics are sourced directly from Jira story ticket data and updated automatically.

Sprint Readiness reflects how many stories are ready in upcoming sprints based on statuses like "Ready for Development" and "To Do". Backlog Health measures grooming coverage across future sprints.
